Why you'll love Solid as a developer

Focus on your idea

Build applications decoupled from data, and avoid maintaining a separate back-end and what comes with it (logging, monitoring...)

Go directly to the data

Access the data you need straight from the user, without depending on any API provider-specific feature.

Be part of a community

Reuse other apps' data, and let other reuse yours.

Contribute to the whole stack

Be empowered to read and improve the specification: the Solid standards are open, as well as some of their implementations.

Get started with Solid

Build Solid web apps

Enable users of your apps to make most of their data.

Host Solid pods

Become a Pod provider to help users store their data, or self-host your Pod.

If you're not sure which one of those paths is right for you, and just want to start playing with Solid try to develop your first Web app. Alternatively, you can read more about Solid or search the FAQs. Once you are familiar with Solid, you can check out the job board.
