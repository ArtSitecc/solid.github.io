This Week in Solid

Jan 1, 2019

Hand-picked Solid updates delivered to you.

Stay up to date with events, learning resources, and recent developments put together by the Solid team.

You can always check here for the most current issue as well as find a record of past issues. The Solid Manager will manually send a message to the Gitter solid/chat every week as well as to the mailing list of the W3C Solid Community Group which you will receive automatically when you join the W3C Solid Community Group.

Next Up

We’re currently working on next week’s edition. You can contribute by making a Pull Request!

This Year in Solid 2019 is being put together to be published on the 18th of December to give a recap of the highlights. Submit pull requests if you would like something to be mentioned.
